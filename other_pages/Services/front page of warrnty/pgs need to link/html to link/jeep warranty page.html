Jeep Warranty

1. Jeep Extended Warranty:

Jeep offers extended warranties through Mopar, which can provide coverage for up to 84 months or a specific mileage limit. These extended warranties can cover vehicle part repairs and replacements.

2. Transferable Coverage:

Most Jeep extended warranty plans are transferable to subsequent owners for a fee of $50. This means that if you sell your Jeep, the new owner can benefit from the remaining warranty coverage.

3. Trip Interruption Coverage:

Some Jeep extended warranty plans include trip interruption coverage. This means that if your Jeep breaks down while you're on a trip, the warranty can help cover expenses such as lodging, meals, and transportation.

4. Multiple Coverage Levels:

Mopar offers extended Jeep warranties at two levels: Maximum Care and Added Care Plus. Maximum Care covers around 5,000 components, while Added Care Plus covers around 850 components.
